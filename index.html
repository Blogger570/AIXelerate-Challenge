About the Challenge

Empowering the Next Generation of AI Builders.

The AIXelerate Challenge is a student-led AI competition for high schoolers ages 14–18. Participants build passion-driven projects that tackle real-world problems using artificial intelligence.

Beginner-friendly, completely free, and structured to guide you step-by-step — from a raw idea all the way to a live presentation in front of expert judges.

Judging Criteria

How You're Scored.

100 core points with bonus opportunities for outstanding work.

Problem & Impact

25 pts

Is the problem real and meaningful? Who benefits and does it actually matter?

AI Understanding & Use

25 pts

Is AI genuinely needed? Clearly explained without buzzwords?

Execution / Prototype

25 pts

Did you build something real? Does it work, even at a basic level?

FAQ

Common Questions.

Do I need coding experience to participate?+
No coding experience is required. The AIXelerate Challenge is designed for beginners. You'll learn as you go, and our resources guide you through understanding and applying AI without writing complex code.
Can I enter as a solo student?+
Absolutely! You can enter as an individual or as part of a team. There are no restrictions on team size for the first two stages.
Is there a registration fee?+
The AIXelerate Challenge is 100% free to enter. We believe financial barriers should never stop a great idea from getting into the world.
What kind of AI projects qualify?+
Any project that genuinely uses AI to solve a real-world problem qualifies — mental health, education, environment, accessibility, and more. AI must be a meaningful part of the solution, not just a buzzword.
How does the mentor program work?+
Mentors are matched with student teams in Stage 2 for guidance on AI implementation, pitch prep, and ethics. Sessions are virtual and schedule-flexible.
When is the competition?+
The 2027 season is open now. Sign up and we'll send you the full timeline and key dates directly to your inbox.
